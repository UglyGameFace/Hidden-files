## Local patterns beat universal advice

Markdown timing varies by store, department, staffing, and inventory. Treat online claims as a starting hypothesis—not a schedule.

## Build a two-week observation log

Visit at a few different times and record only what you can verify.

```text
Tuesday, 7:20 PM
Bakery: several 40% stickers
Prepared food: no markdowns
Meat: small clearance section, use-by tomorrow
```

After several visits, look for patterns by department.

## Safety first

Check package integrity, temperature, use-by dates, and storage instructions. Freeze eligible food promptly when you will not use it in time.

## Price-per-unit check

A clearance sticker does not guarantee the lowest unit price. Compare weight, package size, and a store-brand alternative.

## Plan around what you will use

Prioritize freezable proteins, bread, and ingredients that fit meals already on your plan. Waste can erase the entire discount.
