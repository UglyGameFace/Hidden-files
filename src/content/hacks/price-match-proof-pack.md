## Match the exact product

Confirm the model number, size, color, quantity, condition, seller, and fulfillment method. Similar packaging is not an exact match.

## Capture live evidence

Keep the competitor product page open and record the current time. The item usually needs to be in stock and immediately purchasable.

## Read the current policy

Check eligible competitors, marketplace exclusions, member-only prices, bundles, coupons, limited-time events, and whether the request must happen before or after purchase.

## Ask clearly

A clean request sounds like this:

> Could you check whether this exact model qualifies for your current price-match policy? It is sold and shipped by the eligible retailer and shows in stock.

## Accept the answer

Do not pressure staff to override policy. If the match is denied, verify the reason and compare the normal purchase options.
