## The deal

Eligible GoPuff accounts may be able to use the promo code **`STARBUCKS10`** on Starbucks items ordered through GoPuff.

The code is intended to take up to **$10 off** an eligible order. Your final total can still include taxes, fees, delivery charges, or other costs, so the checkout total may be higher than expected.

> Promo availability can vary by account, location, order type, and date. Confirm the discount at checkout before placing the order.

## How to use it

1. Sign in to your GoPuff account or create an account if you do not already have one.
2. Open the Starbucks section in GoPuff.
3. Add eligible items while keeping the merchandise subtotal near or below $10.
4. Continue to checkout.
5. Enter the promo code:

```text
STARBUCKS10
```

6. Confirm that the discount appears before submitting the order.
7. Review the final total, including any taxes and service or delivery fees.

## What to expect

If the full discount applies, the Starbucks item subtotal may be reduced by as much as $10. Depending on local fees and taxes, the final total may still be a few dollars rather than completely free.

## Important notes
-CREATE NEW ACCOUNTS TO REPEAT THE DEAL!
- Use a VPN and incognito mode to make multiple accounts.
- Make sure the discount is visible before placing the order.
- Check whether there is a minimum spend or item restriction.
- Delivery fees, service fees, small-order fees, and tips may not be discounted.
- Pickup may produce a lower final total when available.
- Do not assume the code will work for every account or location.

## Account and promo rules

Retailers can cancel orders, revoke credits, or restrict accounts when promo terms are abused.
