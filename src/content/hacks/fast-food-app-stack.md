## Start with the meal, not the coupon

Choose what you actually want to eat, then compare the cheapest valid way to order it. A discount on extra food is still extra spending.

## The three-price check

Compare these before checkout:

1. The restaurant app pickup total.
2. The restaurant website pickup total.
3. A delivery marketplace total after all fees and tips.

Pickup usually provides the cleanest comparison because delivery markups can hide the value of a promotion.

## Loyalty decision

Use points when the cents-per-point value is strong and the points are at risk of expiring. Save them when a cash promotion already covers the same item and the app permits only one reward.

```text
Cash price of reward: $6.49
Points required:      1,500
Value per 1,000:      $4.33
```

## Checkout audit

Before placing the order, verify the store location, pickup method, quantities, substitutions, taxes, and final total.

## Avoid false savings

Do not compare a discounted combo against the price of unrelated individual items. Compare the exact food you planned to buy.
