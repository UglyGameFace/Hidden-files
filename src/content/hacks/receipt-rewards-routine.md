## Why a routine matters

Receipt apps are easy to forget and easy to overvalue. A short routine keeps them useful without turning a small reward into a part-time job.

## The workflow

### Capture

Photograph the full receipt while it is flat and readable. Include the store, date, total, and qualifying lines.

### Submit

Use only apps whose terms allow the receipt and submit accurate information. Do not alter receipts or submit the same receipt where duplicate use is prohibited.

### Verify

Check that the expected item or trip credit appears. Fix rejected scans promptly while the receipt is still eligible.

### Archive

Keep the physical or digital receipt until the return period and reward review period have passed.

## Weekly review

Once a week, check pending balances and expiring rewards. Cash out only through official redemption methods and watch for minimum thresholds.

## Time-value rule

A $0.25 reward is not worth a ten-minute dispute. Put your effort into higher-value rebates and repeatable offers.
